For Rania

From Judas Romanovski

My Exquisite Contradiction,

You breathe in verses laced with venom, and yet you live for more — my exquisite contradiction. I watched from the shadows, watched you tilt your chin at the world like it hadn't already broken you in all the beautiful ways I admire.

Every defiance in your gaze was a dare — and I, Judas Romanovski, have never backed away from a challenge.

Rania — a name that now stains every breath I take. You wear darkness like silk, and chaos like perfume. I don't celebrate birthdays. But yours? Yours is a requiem I'll sing until the stars bleed.

I don't offer flowers or foolish candles. I offer obsession carved into bone. Madness wrapped in devotion. On this day, the world was cursed enough to birth you, and blessed enough to never recover.

So here it is — not a gift, but a promise: The next time you dream of me, let it consume you. Let it ruin you. Let it make you mine.

Happy Birthday, my deadly beauty.

— Judas
